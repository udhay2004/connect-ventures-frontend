Developed by Dr. Anil Gupta, Chairman, Connect Ventures. The 5C model is an integrated expansion blueprint that addresses all five dimensions of global commerce simultaneously — making Connect Ventures your single point of accountability.

Why the 5C model exists

Global expansion fails when companies specialise in only one or two dimensions

Most consultancies handle incorporation OR tax OR compliance. Most logistics firms handle shipping but not corporate filings. Most immigration lawyers handle visas but not banking. The result: Indian businesses juggle 6–8 separate agencies, none of whom sees the full picture. The 5C model eliminates this fragmentation by tying together strategy, operations, networks, trade compliance, and co-creation under one roof.
