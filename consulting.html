C2 — Consulting

The backbone of every international expansion

The C2 Consulting module is the largest service area — covering all the legal, financial, and compliance infrastructure that enables an Indian business to operate legally and compliantly in a foreign market.

Foreign company incorporation

Register in USA (LLC/C-Corp), UK (Ltd), UAE (Free Zone/Mainland), Singapore (Pte Ltd), Canada and 35+ other jurisdictions — fully remote, no travel or visa required. We advise on optimal structure, handle all filings, and manage registered offices.

International taxation and DTAA

DTAA benefit planning, Form 5472 annual filing ($25,000 penalty if missed), transfer pricing documentation, VAT/GST registration, FEMA compliance, foreign tax credit optimisation, and LUT filing for services export.

Cross-border banking

Corporate bank accounts in USA (Mercury, Relay), UK, UAE, Singapore without travelling or an SSN. EIN facilitation, documentation preparation, and ongoing banking relationship management.

Secretarial compliance

Annual returns, FinCEN BOI reports, Companies House filings, board minutes, director KYC, statutory registers, and confirmation statements — proactive deadline management across all jurisdictions.

IPR protection

International trademark via Madrid Protocol (critical for Amazon Brand Registry), PCT patent filing, GI tag registration, Hague System design patents, and copyright registration across 47+ jurisdictions.

Trade finance

CGTSME collateral-free credit for MSMEs, ECGC export credit guarantee, BCM instruments, NBFC lending via L&T Finance, invoice discounting, and UHNI unsecured finance facilities.

Employer on Record (EOR)

Hire employees in USA, UK, UAE, Singapore and India without a local entity. We manage payroll, employment contracts, tax withholding, benefits, and statutory compliance.

Localization services

GDPR EU Representative appointment, website translation, packaging redesign for regulatory compliance, product adaptation, marketing localisation for each target market. 20+ languages.

Market research (BIR)

Business Intelligence Reports, sector-specific research, competitor landscape mapping, consumer behaviour studies, regulatory environment analysis, and market sizing for entry decisions.
