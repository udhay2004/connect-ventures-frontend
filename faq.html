⚖️ Tax & Compliance

DTAA (Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement) prevents the same income being taxed in both India and the destination country. For USA: dividends taxed at 15% (reduced from 30%); interest at 15%; royalties at 15%. To claim DTAA: get Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) from Indian IT dept, file Form 10F, provide to foreign payer. Connect Ventures handles TRC applications and DTAA benefit planning.

Also searched: DTAA India USA | double taxation India | how to avoid double tax India | DTAA benefits India

Form 5472 is an IRS annual information return for foreign-owned US single-member LLCs. Reports all transactions between foreign owner and LLC. Due April 15 (September 15 with extension). Penalty for non-filing: $25,000 per form per year — automatically assessed, no warning. This is the most dangerous compliance trap for Indian-owned US entities. Connect Ventures includes Form 5472 in all annual compliance packages.
